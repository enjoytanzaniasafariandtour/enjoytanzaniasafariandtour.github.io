Itinerary information
ARRIVAL DAY
Please tell us your DATE and TIME of arrival

Pick up from Airport to the hotel at Arusha, take shower and rest, after that briefing about safari will follow.

DAY 1 ― ARUSHA-TARANGIRE NATIONAL PARK
Travel time: 2h 30min
Distance: 130km

Pick up from Arusha and transfer to Tarangire national park.
We arrive in time for game viewing. The park has varied habitats that attracts a wide of variety of animals, including one of the largest concentrations of elephant and the biggest baobao trees, plus much more. After game viewing we will go to a hotel at Karatu.

DAY 2 ― KARATU-SERENGETI NATIONAL PARK
Game viewing travel time: 5h
Distance: 1O5km

After breakfast we will head towards Serengeti National park, via the beautiful farmland of Karatu and Ngorongoro Conservation Area.
Leaving the highlands behind us, we will descend into the heart of Serengeti National park.
We will head to the central area of the park known as Seronera area. It is one of the best places to spot many species of animal and plants, because of the Seronera river crosses here and provides a valuable source of water to this area.
We arrive in time for lunch at a picnic site. After lunch we will enjoy an afternoon game drive in Serengeti National park. As Serengeti is very Wide Park we will have two days there in order to explore more places. We will have Dinner and an overnight stay in the Seronera area.

DAY 3 ― SERENGETI NATIONAL PARK
Only game viewing

After breakfast we do a game viewing drive along the loops into the plain south of the Seronera River and it is possible to see lots of animals like lion, cheetah and antelopes. The visitor center close to Serengeti Seronera lodge is good to visit after the morning game drive. There is a shops where cold drinks and snacks can be found. In the afternoon we will go around Maasai kopjes because this area will attract a large number of animas. This will be the day to enjoy Serengeti ecosystem. We will see a larger group of wildebeest, zebra, giraffe, Thomson gazelle, buffalo and other animals. Serengeti kopjes provide shade during sun day and during rainy season animals hide in the rock. This makes kopjes a particularly good for spotting wildlife in the dry season.

DAY 4 ― SERENGETI NATIONAL PARK-NGORONGORO CRATER
Travel time: 3hrs
Distance: 99km

After breakfast at the lodge we start to go at Ngorongoro crater.
On the way we will stop at Oldupai Gorge we will get time to see historical information about the foot print of the earlier man and the chain of the human evolution. Our trip will continue to the crater where we will have lunch then after we will enjoy afternoon game viewing.
Ngorongoro crater is about 600 meters deep and inside there is lots of animals like hyenas, jackals, cheetah, lion, hippo, buffalo, and if we are lucky we will see black rhinos.
In the crater there is also a beautiful lake called Lake Magadi, here there is a large number of flamingo and other water birds.

DAY 5 ― NGORONGORO-ARUSHA
Non game-viewing travel time: 4hrs
Distance: 190 km

This is the last day in the park we will enjoy breakfast which will take us towards Arusha. We will go back to the hotel, then relax and wait for debriefing, then after that we will discuss about two days in Zanzibar.
